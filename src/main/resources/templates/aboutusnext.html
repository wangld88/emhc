Initially, EMHC's target market was health sciences students, but has expanded to include staff in patient care facilities and others whose work environment may bring them into contact with infectious respiratory agents. Serving the GTA and beyond, the company goal is to be recognised as a premier provider of fit test services.

What makes us unique and separates us from the rest is that we know healthcare. We know how things really work in the stressful world of healthcare. We can anticipate and identify what your needs are, but more importantly, we feel we are helping to protect our own! It's personal with us!

Some do not provide the necessary training behind using a respirator.   You need to be aware. You need to be concerned. As an employer you need to ensure your personnel are protected.

EMH Consulting is here to help you. Give us a call and let us take steps to ensure we offer the best personal protection possible for your staff.
