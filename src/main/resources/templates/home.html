FIT TESTING | ABOUT US | WHY CHOOSE EMH  

The purpose of wearing an N95 respirator is to provide the individual with respiratory protection in the workplace.

An N95 respirator by itself does not provide respiratory protection.

Fit testing to an N95 respirator coupled with appropriate education and training provides reasonable assurance that respiratory protection can be achieved.
 

We utilize the highest standards in fit testing – complying with the Canadian Centre for Occupational Health and Safety (CCOHS), Canadian Standards Association (CSA), Ontario Safety Association for Community and Healthcare (OSACH), Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) and the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH).
