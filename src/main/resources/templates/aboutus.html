EMH Consulting was formed in 2000 with a simple mandate - "Offer quality and professional services to the healthcare industry".

Beginning as a one person operation EMH Consulting began providing organizational consulting services to Departments of Nursing, in the Greater Toronto Area. Word of mouth of a superior service grew - it was one of the few Canadian consulting firms who actually had practical healthcare experience, and the operation thrived.   And so things may have continued that way if it had not been for the emergence of SARS in 2003.

From SARS grew a new industry of Respiratory Mask Fitting companies with varying competencies. Coming from a healthcare perspective, EMH Consulting was concerned with the varying competencies of respirator or mask fitting services and decided to do something about it! We believe we are the MOUSE THAT ROARED .   A small company but with a loud voice to spread the word that healthcare workers are in danger of having inadequate respirator training.

In 2003 EMH Consulting launched its own respiratory mask fitting program. EMH Consulting became and still is the only Ontario Fit Test provider with a professional health care background.   Further, it is the knowledge and skill acquired during an extensive nursing career that separates us from the rest and offers a far superior and more healthcare specific perspective.
