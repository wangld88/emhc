Q.   How do I know which respirator to use?

A.   You only use the respirator you are certified by 'fit testing" to use, this is identified on your Fit Test Card.

Q.   How do I know when to wear a respirator and when to wear a surgical mask.

A.   Signs indicating what personal protective equipment to use are posted outside of patient rooms or the entrance to patient care units.

Q.   What do I do if my model of respirator is not available and I am told to wear another respirator that I have not been fit tested to?

A.   Request that your model be made available for you or that you be fit tested to another respirator. You never wear a respirator for which you have not been fit tested.
